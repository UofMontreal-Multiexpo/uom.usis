Datasets

The main USIS datasets:

  • USIS_inspections
  • USIS_sheets
  • USIS_measures

Other USIS datasets:

  • USIS_agencies
  • USIS_establishment_names
  • USIS_establishments
  • USIS_exposure_types
  • USIS_inspection_types
  • USIS_locations
  • USIS_measure_units
  • USIS_sample_types
  • USIS_scopes
  • USIS_substances
  • USIS_workplaces

The official version of 2002 of the North American Industry Classification System (considering only classes used in the United States): NAICS

The official version of 1980 of the U.S. census occupational classification system: OCC

The official version of 1987 of the U.S. Standard Industrial Classification: SIC

The states and other inhabited territories of the United States: US_territories

Functions

Join all datasets into a single data frame:

  • join_USIS
  • join_IMIS
  • join_OIS

Extract measures:

  • get_IMIS_measures
  • get_OIS_measures

Extract specific data:

  • get_original_db
  • get_establishment_names
